About Me

I am a PhD student in Applied Mathematics at CMAP (École Polytechnique) since October 2024, affiliated with the PEIPS and MERGE research teams. My doctoral research is supervised by Sylvie Méléard and Viet-Chi Tran and focuses on the mathematical modeling of horizontal gene transfer in biology, specifically plasmid conjugation in bacteria.

My work combines various methodologies, including Markov processes, time reversal, spinal decomposition, integro-differential equations, and numerical simulation. My research interests are primarily centered on evolutionary theory, with a focus on evolutionary trade-offs, mutualism, as well as the dynamics of cooperation and cheating.
